Ask Me Anything App

A simple and clean interface that connects the team with leadership. It is intended to be available all the time for anyone to send questions that will be answered during a townhall or event.

This specific project was built on a PowerApps by the development team and was defined to be embedded on a Microsoft Teams Channel, making it easy for everyone to access. The theme color chosen matches MS Teams by design, to make it feel native.
